Food and Me

I have always enjoyed cooking. My mother often held a home party for families and friends.
Since she had to cook for many people, I always helped her. As I participate in the activities, I came to know how fun it is to cook. It can change a daily necessity in to fun special moment. Tiny little change in the cooking process will bring a major change in the cooking and makes it unique. Fortunately, my wife loves cooking as well. We always cook for each other and dinner time will become important time to show my love to her. Cooking can bring more joy into your life.
